About the Osasco Chamber of Commerce

Learn more about our mission and vision.

Our Mission, Vision, and Values

Mission

To empower local businesses, foster economic growth, and build a thriving community in Osasco.

Vision

To be the leading organization driving innovation, collaboration, and success for businesses in Osasco.

Values

Integrity, collaboration, innovation, and commitment to the community.

Our Main Achievements

  • Organized over 100 networking events connecting local businesses.
  • Advocated for policies that improved the local business environment.
  • Supported over 500 small businesses with resources and training.
  • Launched a digital directory to promote member businesses online.

Benefits of Joining the Chamber

  • Access to exclusive networking events and workshops.
  • Increased visibility through our business directory.
  • Advocacy for your business at the local and regional levels.
  • Opportunities to collaborate with other businesses.
  • Discounts on chamber-sponsored events and services.
